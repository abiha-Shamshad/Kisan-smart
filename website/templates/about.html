Our Mission

Bridging the Technology Gap in Agriculture

Millions of smallholder farmers in Pakistan lack access to modern agricultural advice. Kisan Smart changes that by delivering AI-driven recommendations directly to the farmer — in their language, at zero cost.

AI-Powered Analysis

ML model analyzes N-P-K, pH, crop type for 94% accurate fertilizer recommendations.

Weather Alerts

Live Open-Meteo data generates agricultural hazard warnings — heatwaves, heavy rain, humidity.

Pest Risk Scoring

Father & Inspiration

"My father, Shamshad Ali, has been farming for over 30 years. I watched him struggle — buying the wrong fertilizers, losing crops to diseases he couldn't identify, and making costly mistakes that no AI could help him with because no one built it for farmers like him. Kisan Smart is my answer to that."

He taught me that every grain of wheat carries the weight of a family's hope. This project is dedicated to him and every hardworking farmer who deserves better tools.
